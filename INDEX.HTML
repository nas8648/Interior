About

At Interior, we believe that great design is more than just aesthetics — it's about creating spaces that inspire, uplift, and reflect the personality and lifestyle of those who live or work in them. With a passion for creativity and an eye for detail, we specialize in transforming interiors into functional, elegant, and timeless environments.

From modern minimalism to classic luxury, our team of experienced designers works closely with clients to bring their vision to life. Whether it’s a cozy home makeover, a stylish office revamp, or a large-scale commercial project, we ensure every design is tailored to suit your taste, needs, and budget.

With years of experience in the industry, a commitment to quality, and a love for innovative design, we take pride in delivering exceptional results that go beyond expectations. Your space is a reflection of you — let’s make it unforgettable.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping Process

Once your order is finalized and payment is confirmed, we begin the production and quality-check process. Delivery takes between 7–21 business days depending on the product. You’ll receive timely tracking updates via email or SMS.

Delivery Coverage

We deliver across all major cities in India. Remote locations may take longer or have extra shipping costs. For international orders, please contact us for availability and custom options.

Packaging & Handling

Your items are securely packaged using high-quality protective materials. Our team ensures safe delivery and on-site installation (if applicable) with utmost care.

Shipping Charges

Free shipping on all orders above ₹50,000. Standard delivery fees apply to smaller or remote-location orders, which will be clearly communicated prior to shipping.

Delivery & Return Policy

Report any damage or incorrect item within 48 hours for replacement. Since most items are made to order, returns are accepted only for damaged or defective deliveries.
